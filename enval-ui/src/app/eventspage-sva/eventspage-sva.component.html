Senior Value Analyst (SVA) Course Overview

The course is designed for industry professionals and academic faculty for gaining competency as VM trainers. The batches are conducted both for organisations and individual participants from multiple organisations.

Benefits

  • Eligible as a trainer to conduct VM training for Student Value Practitioner (SVP) Course after getting Instructor License from INVEST
  • SVA-qualified professional can lead Value studies
  • SVA can also conduct awareness sessions and workshops within and outside the institution or organization

Eligibility

  • Industry professionals must have 4 years of experience in practicing VM
  • College / University faculty have no specific pre-qualification criteria

This course include:

  • 24 hours of training by INVEST Licensed Instructor
  • 1 Large/2 Small VM projects/study
  • Access on mobile and TV
  • Certification of completion

Content and Coverage

  • Fundamentals of VM
  • Application of VM across industry, government, academia and social sector
  • Need, methods and processes for contextualising content for specific disciplines for SVP training
  • Orientation on facilitation skills
  • Preparation for SVA examination, certification and renewal

Training Methodology

  • Interactive lectures – online and offline depending on modality opted
  • Case Studies
  • Individual practice cases
  • Group exercises

Examination and Certification

  • Training is conducted by ENVAL, while exam is scheduled and conducted by INVEST.
  • Exam comprises 2 objective papers of 100 marks each, 70% passing marks in each paper.
  • Certificate awarded to the successful candidates is valid for 4 years and has to be renewed thereafter.
