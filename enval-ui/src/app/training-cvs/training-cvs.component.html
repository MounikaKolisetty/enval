Course Overview

CVS is a professional certification that recognizes individuals who have demonstrated an expert level experience and knowledge in the practice of Value Methodology. It is the highest level of certification in value methodology from SAVE international

Benefits

  • Gain eligibility for conducting all certification training programs
  • Lead VE studies and large-scale projects independently

Eligibility

  • VMA in good standing, and completed Value Methodology Fundamentals VMF-2 Training, as per SAVE guidelines
  • Completed 6 VE Studies / projects
  • Received requisite Credit Points (CPs) according to the SAVE International Guidelines.

Process and Examination

  • Undertake VMF 1 course and pass the exam to gain VMA Certification.
  • Complete an approved VMF 2 course. The VMF 2 may not be taken until the applicant participates in a minimum of two value studies (minimum of 24 hours each) following the completion of the VMF 1 training and certification.
  • Select guide who is a CVS® in good standing.
  • Submit CVS® Application Package to INVEST.
  • Receive approval of the application.
  • Write 6 research papers based on the VE Studies / projects and submit your claim in the form of an application and supporting documents for having completed all requirements to the INVEST Certification Board.
  • INVEST Certification board will notify after acceptance of application for examination.
  • Offline Exam will be conducted by INVEST, comprising 5 papers
  • Pass the exam of 5 papers in 3.5 hours with min 70% marks in each paper

Certification and Maintenance

  • Certification is awarded to a successful candidate after passing all 5 papers of the CVS exam.
  • Total 3 exams are scheduled and conducted by the institution / college, with 70% passing marks in each exam.
  • Biannual maintenance fee has to be paid to INVEST.
