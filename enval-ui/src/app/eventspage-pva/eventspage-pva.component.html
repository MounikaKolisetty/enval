Professional Value Analyst (PVA) Course Overview

The course is offered to professionals from all sectors, including academia. The training programs are conducted both at organisational level and individual participants from multiple organisations.

Benefits

  • Lead VM project in any organisation
  • Promote VM within and outside organisation
  • Conduct sessions or co-facilitate Trainings along with Licensed Trainers
  • Gain in-depth understanding of concept of VM
  • Develop expertise for application of VM principles and technique for improving products, processes, services or projects of the organisations or clients
  • Eligibility to conduct session/s on specific modules of VM in a training or workshop conducted by expert licensed trainer

Eligibility

  • Industry professionals must have 4 years of experience in practicingÂ VM
  • College / University faculty have no specific pre-qualification criteria

This course include:

  • 32 hours of training by INVEST Licensed Instructor
  • Project Work
  • Access on mobile and TV
  • Certification of completion

Content and Coverage

  • Fundamentals of VM
  • Application of VM across industry, government, academia and social sector
  • Orientation on specific skills for leading VM Projects
  • Orientation on facilitation skills
  • Preparation for PVA examination, certification and renewal

Training Methodology

  • Interactive lectures - online and offline depending on modality opted
  • Case studies
  • Intensive group work with focus on VM project management
  • In case of organizational training with batches of participants from the same organisation, the workshops are mostly conducted on live projects based on specific objectives.

Examination and Certification

  • The training is conducted by ENVAL and a Training Certificate is awarded to participants successfully completing all modules.
  • Examination is scheduled and conducted by INVEST, comprising 2 objective papers of 100 marks each, 70% passing marks in each paper.
  • Certification is awarded to the successful candidates by INVEST. It is valid for 4 years and has to be renewed thereafter.
