Course Overview

Value Methodology Associate (VMA) is a common certification for students, trainers, academicians and practitioners with eligibility of participating in VM projects.

Benefits

  • The Certification is valid at international level
  • Candidates can participate in any VM project as team members

Eligibility

  • Any student, academician and professional
  • There is no restriction on specialisation and profession

Content

  • Value Methodology Training (VMT) Module
  • Fundamentals of VM
  • Applications of VM

Training Methodology

  • Interactive lectures - online and offline
  • Individual assignments
  • Case studies and group work
  • Short projects at institutional / college level

Examination and Certification

  • The training is conducted by ENVAL, while Certification is awarded by SAVE International.
  • The participant is awarded Certification for successful completion of VMF1 Training, which is a must for taking the VMA exam.
  • Exam comprises 60 multiple choice questions, to be completed in 90 minutes, with 70% passing marks.
  • Certification must be renewed every 2 years.
