Student Value Practitioner (SVP) Course Overview

ENVAL has developed modular training program for the SVP Certification. It is an entry-level Certification for students of all disciplines and is offered as an elective subject as a part of the curriculum at the graduate and post-graduate levels of any stream.

The training is conducted through institutions opting for offering it as an elective paper / coursework. Certification is provided by INVEST.

Benefits

  • Develop competency for application of VM in academics and career
  • SVP-Certified students can lead student projects in their own college/university
  • Participate as team members in external VM projects
  • SVP candidates can have an edge over the competition in placement
  • Pperform better in the respective organization after securing employment through the application of the VM technique.

Eligibility

Students enrolled in full-time/part-time graduate / post-graduate course

This course include:

  • 20 hours of classroom training
  • Project Work
  • Access on mobile and TV
  • Certification of completion

Content and Coverage

  • Fundamentals of VM
  • Application of VM in India and at global level
  • Importance of VM in academic activities
  • Conducting academic VM projects

Training Methodology

  • Interactive lectures - online and offline
  • Individual assignments
  • Case studies and group work
  • Short projects at institutional / college level

Examination and Certification

  • Training is conducted by ENVAL.
  • Total 3 exams are scheduled and conducted by the institution / college, with 70% passing marks in each exam.
  • Certification is awarded by INVEST
